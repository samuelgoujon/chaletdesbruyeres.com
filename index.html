FAQ


Location of home?

7 km from Montfort l'Amaury — one of most beautiful villages in France. many celebrities live (and have lived) in and around the area, Brigitte Bardot, Philippe Starck, Chantal Thomas, Florent Pagny, family Lagardere (St leger en Yvelines, Family Pinault (Grosrouvre), Guillaume Canet, Anoulh, Ravel, Victor Hugo, Anne de Bretagne... Proust talks about the Stained glass windows of the church in "À la recherche du temps perdu".

What's included in the sale? White goods? Curtains? Wood burner?

Everything, furniture negotiable. The kitchen, custom made in solid oak, with Italian cupboards and Ilve cooker will be left.

What's the council tax band?

About 3000 euros" taxe d'habitation" and the same for "Taxe Foncier" per annum.

Have there been any subsidence problems?

Not in the 150 years of its existence.

Transportation?

Paris easily accessible by car (40 mins — 45km to Porte d'Auteuil) or public transport — train station 15 minutes away — Paris Montparanasse 35 mins.

Deauville and the Normandy coast an hour and a half away.

Bus for schools in Rambouillet and International school in Montfort — 5 mins walk.

Changing country of work.

What renovations have been done?

Work carried out 2004/2005 (all bills and invoices can been sent).

Are there any parking issues?

No.

How old is the boiler and when was it last inspected?

Boilers serviced every year. 2 boilers (one in main house - de Dietrich, one in dependance) installed 2004/2005. Fuel oil. 2 tanks 5000 litres and 3000 litres.

How long have you lived there?

Since 2005.

2 fires and numerous, possibility for other fires (chimneys conserved). Must be correctly swept every year but used regularly. The fireplace in the salon is the most used.
